What makes Neiro different

10-second capture

Hit record. 10 seconds. Done. No editing, no setup — just the sound you heard right now.

Layer your own sounds

Add today's 10 seconds on top of what you recorded before. Hear your archive grow into something bigger.

Loop & listen

Your mix loops seamlessly. Hear how your sound fits — or clashes — with what came before.

No install needed

Neiro is a PWA. Open it in your browser, record, post. Works on any device.

Why I built this
"I kept hearing interesting sounds — a café, a rainstorm, a guitar riff — and wanting to share them with someone who would add to them, not just listen — for now, that someone is the you of tomorrow. Every music app I tried wanted me to make a finished song. Neiro is the opposite: start something unfinished, and see where it goes."
CHUNK-jp developer · independent builder
